ABOUT

ShopLaptop is a store selling laptops and other IT products. We offer a wide range of professional-grade computer equipment at much lower market prices.

The level of modern top-end computers and laptops makes it unnecessary to constantly purchase new equipment. Since most applications and tasks no longer require the latest hardware, we offer users an environmentally and economically viable alternative to a new device - a post-leased product with a 12-month warranty as good as new.

We are focused on selling high quality notebooks from top brands (Lenovo, Dell, HP and Panasonic). The models we sell are exceptionally high-quality business-class devices that have been created for long-term and comfortable work.
